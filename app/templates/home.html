The Homeless System Simulation Game is designed to help communities understand how the homeless assistance system works. The simulation is a very simplified version of how homelessness assistance systems work, and was designed using actual data for outcomes of programs in different communities across the country. The game encourages thought about your local homelessness assistance system, the impact of decisions and strategies to reduce homelessness, and system-wide outcomes. Questions regarding the game should be directed to thecenter@naeh.org.
